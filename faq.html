Help & Support

Frequently asked questions and support resources.

How do I lodge a report?

Go to the "Lodge a Report" section from the main menu. Fill in the details of the issue, attach photos if needed, and click Submit.

Where can I find information about upcoming events?

All upcoming community events can be found in the "Community Events" section.

What kind of issues can I report?

Residents can report community-related concerns such as broken streetlights, water leakage, cleanliness issues, security concerns, noise complaints and others.

Is my report anonymous?

Yes, you can choose to submit your report anonymously. However, providing your details can help the management follow up more effectively.

How long does it take for a report to be addressed?

Reports are typically reviewed within 2-3 working days. Urgent issues may receive faster attention.
