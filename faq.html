Help & Support

Frequently asked questions and support resources.

How do I lodge a report?

Simply click the "Report" button in the main menu, fill in the required details (location, issue type, description), and submit. You’ll receive a confirmation once it's successfully sent.

Where can I find information about upcoming events?

Event details are posted on the Community Notes page.

What kind of issues can I report?

You can report anything related to community safety, cleanliness, faulty street lights, broken public facilities, noise complaints, or any disturbances within your area.

Is my report anonymous?

Yes. You can choose to submit your report anonymously. However, providing your contact info may help the admin follow up more effectively.

How long does it take for a report to be addressed?

Reports are typically reviewed within 1-2 working days.
